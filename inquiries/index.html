Private Research Partnerships

The author occasionally undertakes long-horizon research partnerships with individuals committed to the deep study of their own cognitive continuity, legacy, and creative evolution in extended interaction with AI systems.

These partnerships are not consulting, not therapy, not coaching. They are co-investigative engagements grounded in the methodological framework documented in the AUGMANITAI Open Lexicon and the author's published working-paper series.

The partnership format exists because some questions about human-AI cognitive interaction can only be examined seriously over years rather than weeks, in deep contact with a single subject who is willing to commit the time, the attention, and the discretion that the work requires.

Selectivity

A small number of new partnerships are accepted per year. Selection is by mutual fit rather than by availability. The work is too time-intensive for any other model.

Discretion

All partnerships operate under bilateral non-disclosure. No partner is named publicly. No engagement is referenced in subsequent publications without explicit consent.

Duration

Engagements are measured in years, not weeks. Partial or short-term inquiries are not accepted. The methodology requires long observation horizons.

Framework

Grounded in the AUGMANITAI methodological corpus and the PERMANITAI performance-factor framework. Bilingual (EN/DE) on request. Documentation under bilateral NDA.

§ 01What this is not

This is not a service. It is not a product. It is not coaching, mentoring, therapy, advice, or executive briefing. It is not available on a subscription, retainer, or hourly basis. There is no published fee structure because each engagement is structured individually around what the research question actually requires.

§ 02What this is

A bilateral research partnership in which the author and the partner together examine, document, and refine the partner's specific cognitive, creative, and continuity questions in sustained interaction with AI systems — using the AUGMANITAI vocabulary as a working frame and producing observations, named patterns, and bilingual documentation as the principal output.

The partner participates as a co-investigator, not as a client. Outputs belong to the partner. Methodological observations may be incorporated, in fully anonymised form, into the public AUGMANITAI corpus.

§ 03Suitable partners

The format suits individuals whose cognitive work is sufficiently developed, sustained, and complex to make a multi-year observation programme meaningful — and whose situation requires the kind of discretion that only bilateral non-disclosure can provide. Most appropriate candidates self-recognise.

Initial inquiry

First contact is by written message to inquiry@augmanitai.com. A short note describing the area of interest is sufficient. A mutual non-disclosure agreement is prepared before any substantive conversation begins.

No fee structure is published; this is determined per engagement. Subsequent steps are arranged once mutual fit is established.

All correspondence is treated as strictly confidential.
