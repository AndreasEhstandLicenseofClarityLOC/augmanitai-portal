Methodological Frame

Holistic Performance Analysis

A cross-domain transfer of high-performance sport-science methodology (25-year Bundesliga tennis coaching lineage) to human-AI interaction analysis. Every observation passes four structural pillars; every term is built on this skeleton.

01 / OBSERVABLE

Detectable in Practice

Each phenomenon must be detectable in real human-AI interaction. No purely speculative entities.

02 / DECOMPOSABLE

Broken into Sub-Phenomena

Complex patterns broken into smaller, distinct, named sub-phenomena. Compositional hierarchy.

03 / MEASURABLE

Operational Quantification

Operational definitions allow quantification, even where the phenomenon is subjective in nature.

04 / CONTROLLABLE

Intentional Intervention

Measured phenomena enter the space of intentional intervention — descriptive, not prescriptive.
